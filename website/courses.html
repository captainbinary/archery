Archery Taster Sessions!

As part of Archery GB's 'Start Archery' week, we proudly hosted an Archery Taster session on Monday, 8th May 2023 at our Medina field.

Despite the unpredictable weather, the day proved to be a resounding success, with participants stepping up to the challenge and discovering the joy of archery. The club's commitment to fostering a love for this ancient sport was further solidified as we eagerly look forward to participating in next year's 'Start Archery Week.'

The remarkable event caught the attention of the Isle of Wight County Press, which featured an article on 27th April 2023.

Beginner Courses!

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of archery with our Archery Beginner Courses, carefully crafted to introduce aspiring archers to the art and sport of archery in a structured and supportive environment.

🎯 Whether you're a complete novice or have some experience under your belt, our classes cater to all skill levels, ensuring everyone feels welcome and encouraged. As you witness your arrows gracefully approach the bullseye, your confidence will soar to new heights!

✨ Guided by our expert instructors, participants delve into the intricacies of archery, mastering proper form, stance, and posture. You'll become well-versed in the art of handling bows and nocks, while emphasizing the utmost importance of safety guidelines and equipment handling protocols for a secure and enjoyable learning experience.

🔧 Our courses focus on building a rock-solid foundation of skills, taking you from the basics of target shooting to more advanced techniques. With personalized guidance and invaluable feedback from our instructors, you'll witness remarkable improvements in your form and accuracy, reaching new levels of proficiency.

🤝 Beyond the individual growth, our courses cultivate a strong sense of camaraderie and community among participants. Connecting with fellow archery enthusiasts allows you to share the exhilaration of the sport, creating lasting friendships and fostering an environment of support and encouragement.

🌟 As the curtain falls on our archery beginner courses, you can expect to possess a firm grasp of archery techniques, showcasing enhanced accuracy in your shooting. Moreover, you'll notice a remarkable boost in your focus, concentration, and a profound appreciation for the timeless art form that is archery.

Join us on this remarkable journey, where arrows meet targets and dreams take flight. Enroll in our Archery Beginner Courses today and unlock your true archer potential!
